Lucazon Launches New Gaming Category

January 15, 2024

Lucazon is excited to announce the launch of our new Gaming category, featuring the latest gaming consoles, accessories, and peripherals. Customers can now find everything they need for their gaming setup in one place, including PlayStation 5 consoles, controllers, and gaming headsets.

"We're thrilled to expand our product offerings into gaming," said the CEO. "This new category represents our commitment to providing customers with a comprehensive shopping experience."

Lucazon Reaches 1 Million Customers Milestone

Lucazon is proud to announce that we've reached one million registered customers. This milestone reflects our commitment to providing exceptional service and quality products.

New Mobile App Launch

November 5, 2023

Lucazon has launched its new mobile application, available on iOS and Android. The app features a streamlined interface, faster checkout, and exclusive mobile-only deals.
