Why Sell on Lucazon?

Join thousands of successful sellers who are growing their businesses on Lucazon. Our platform provides everything you need to reach customers, manage inventory, and process orders efficiently.

  • Flexible Pricing: Competitive commission rates with no hidden fees

How It Works

  1. Create Your Seller Account: Sign up and complete your seller profile
  2. List Your Products: Add product listings with photos, descriptions, and pricing
  3. Manage Inventory: Keep track of stock levels and update availability
  4. Receive Orders: Get notified when customers purchase your products
  5. Fulfill Orders: Ship products directly to customers
  6. Get Paid: Receive payments securely and on time

Commission Structure

Our commission rates are competitive and vary by product category. Typical commission rates range from 8% to 15% of the sale price, with no monthly fees or setup costs.

  • Electronics: 10% commission
  • Clothing: 12% commission
  • Gaming: 10% commission
  • Home & Kitchen: 12% commission
  • Other Categories: 8-15% commission (varies by category)

Requirements

To become a seller on Lucazon, you'll need:

  • Tax identification number
  • Bank account for payments
  • Quality product photos and descriptions
  • Commitment to excellent customer service
